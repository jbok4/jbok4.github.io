RORY MCILROY

Northern Irish professional golfer who is a member of both the European and PGA Tours. He was world number one in the Official World Golf Ranking for 95 weeks.


"We sat there in awe. Nervous energy was replaced with adrenaline. We were fired up."


"Suddenly, instead of cheering for Tiger, I was pulling for an entire European continent to beat him and the rest of the Americans."

Australian professional golfer and PGA Tour member. He is a former World Number 1 in the World Golf Ranking, having first achieved the ranking in September 2015.

PAT PEREZ

Perez was born in Phoenix, Arizona, and is of Mexican-American descent. He experienced his first PGA Tour victory in 2009 at the Bob Hope Classic; he has finished second there twice.


"It was one of the hardest times in my life because I didn’t feel like I had a future. But then something pretty incredible happened. I got a break. A big one. Sometimes in life you just need one person to believe in you..."
